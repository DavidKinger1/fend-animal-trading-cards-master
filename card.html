Golden Retriever

The Golden Retriever is one of the most popular dog breeds in the U.S. The breed’s friendly, tolerant attitude makes him a fabulous family pet.

  • Dog Breed Group:Sporting Dogs
  • Height: 1 foot, 9 inches to 2 feet tall at the shoulder
  • Weight: 55 to 75 pounds
  • Life Span: 10 to 12 years

The Golden Retriever intelligence makes them a highly capable working dog. Golden Retrievers excel at retrieving game for hunters, tracking, sniffing out drugs, and as therapy and assistance dogs. They’re also natural athletes, and do well in dog sports such as agility and competitive obedience.
